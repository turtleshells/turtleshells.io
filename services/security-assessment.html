Application Security

Security Assessment

A comprehensive, framework-driven evaluation of your security posture. We align your tech stack against industry standards to identify control gaps before they become breaches.

Overview

Measuring your security controls against global standards.

A Security Assessment is a holistic evaluation of your application's security posture. Rather than just hunting for bugs, we evaluate the maturity, design, and implementation of your security controls against industry-leading frameworks.

Depending on your specific needs, regulatory requirements, and technical environment, we tailor our assessment using the relevant OWASP framework: ASVS for web applications, MASVS for mobile apps, or AISVS for AI/LLM-integrated systems.

Our Assessment Frameworks

We scope our assessments around the core OWASP pillar that matches your application type.

Web Apps (ASVS)

Evaluate web applications and APIs against the modern OWASP Application Security Verification Standard, ensuring robust defenses across all logical tiers.

Mobile Apps (MASVS)

Assess mobile application security controls aligned with the OWASP Mobile Application Security Verification Standard, covering local storage, auth, and network communication.

AI Systems (AISVS)

Review AI and LLM implementations against the emerging OWASP AI Security Verification Standard, mitigating prompt injection, training data poisoning, and model inversion.

Methodology

Every assessment follows a structured six-phase framework to ensure rigorous evaluation and accurate results.

We align with you to determine if ASVS, MASVS, or AISVS is the appropriate baseline for your project, selecting the specific verification levels required based on your application's risk profile and business function.

We evaluate the high-level security architecture, data flow, trust boundaries, and overarching security controls to ensure a strong foundation before diving into granular implementation details.

We systematically verify the presence and effectiveness of required security controls (e.g., authentication, session management, cryptography) as defined by the chosen framework's requirements.

We document where the application fails to meet the specified framework requirements, identifying missing controls, architectural flaws, or flawed implementations that represent security risks.

We gather concrete documentation, configuration snapshots, and testing artifacts to provide objective proof of compliance or non-compliance for each verification requirement within the framework.

We deliver a comprehensive scorecard detailing your compliance posture, along with specific, actionable recommendations and architectural guidance to close identified gaps and achieve your target security level.

What You Get

A clear, actionable view of your application's alignment with industry standards.

Compliance Matrix

Gap Identification

Strategic Recommendations

Actionable steps and architectural guidance to improve your overall posture and achieve full framework alignment.

Executive Summary

A high-level view of your application's security maturity suitable for leadership, stakeholders, and auditors.
