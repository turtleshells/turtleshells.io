How We Work

From first contact to delivery: our engagement process in six clear steps.

We start with a discovery call to understand your environment, compliance requirements, and risk appetite. We discuss which assets are in scope (applications, APIs, infrastructure), your timeline, and what success looks like for your organization. This conversation ensures we align on objectives before any formal commitment.

We prepare a written proposal that defines the scope of work, methodology, deliverables (e.g. report format, retest policy), and pricing. We clarify assumptions, exclusions, and timelines. You review and we iterate until the scope accurately reflects your needs and we have a clear basis for the next step.

We formalize the engagement with a contract that covers scope, confidentiality, and terms. We assign a dedicated lead as your single point of contact and agree on communication cadence (e.g. status updates, escalation path). Roles, timelines, and access handover are documented so both sides know what to expect.

We hold a kickoff session to confirm scope, access (credentials, environments, documentation), and the project calendar. We align on milestones, report format, and how we will communicate findings during the engagement. This sets the stage for a smooth execution phase with no surprises.

Our team executes the agreed scope, whether penetration testing, threat modeling, code review, or training, following industry-standard methodologies. We provide periodic status updates and, when appropriate, early visibility into critical findings so you can prioritize remediation. We stay within the agreed timeline and scope unless we jointly agree on a change.

We deliver the final report or agreed deliverables and schedule a walkthrough to explain findings, risk ratings, and remediation guidance. We answer your questions and, if needed, discuss retesting, ongoing assessments, or follow-up support so you can maintain and improve your security posture over time.
